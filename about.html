Hello! My name is Alex Jiang (he/him), and I am a second-year undergraduate student at the University of Pennsylvania. I'm currently pursuing a major in mechanical engineering and plan to simultaneously pursue a masters in robotics.

My current life priority is self-improvemnt. Thus far in college, I've had successes and plenty more failures, but, these experiences have taught me valuable lessons and shaped my life philsophy. Here are some of my life principles:

  • Everyday is an opportunity to better yourself.
  • Don't stress about things that are out of your control. If you can fix it, fix it. If you can't, move on.
  • If you think about something you have to do that takes less than 5 minutes, do it immediately.
  • Prioritize creating meaningful friendships over "connecting" with a higher quantity of people
  • If you have an opportunity to try something for the first time, always* say yes.
  • School is overrated. Prioritize personal projects and independent learning over getting perfect grades.
  • Everyone is creative. Actively search for creative experience because school sucks it out of you.
  • Be honest with yourself and others.

This is all easier said than done of course, but I (do my best to) live by these mottos. This website is mainly a portfolio, but I will be publishing a blog soon to share my two cents about whatever comes to mind.
